Our Founding Story

Skylines Sail was born from a simple passion: the love of exploration. Our founder, an avid traveler, spent years navigating the globe, collecting stories and experiences. Frustrated by one-size-fits-all travel packages, they envisioned a company that focused on the heart of travel — personal discovery and authentic connections.

In 2015, that vision became a reality. We started small, with a mission to craft unique journeys that went beyond the guidebooks. Today, Skylines Sail has grown, but our core belief remains the same: travel should be transformative. We handle every detail with care, so you can immerse yourself fully in your adventure.
